Connect With Me

My work is a celebration of the unique beauty that every vulva holds. I founded Vulvaverse to banish the unnecessary shame society has placed upon our bodies, bodies that are, after all, integral to the very essence of life itself. I want to reshape the way we talk about ourselves, breaking down barriers of stigma to foster a space of genuine body knowledge.

Through my workshops, 1:1 coaching, and artistic guidance, I invite you to connect with your body on your own terms. Whether we meet at a market in southern Italy or in a sacred digital space, my goal is the same: to hear proud voices filled with joy.

Despite the challenges of algorithms and shadowbans, I remain dedicated to building a space where everyone who has a vulva can feel seen and inspired. Join me in embracing this part of ourselves with pride, openness, and deep celebration.
